# MLSS 2026 Tübingen — Machine Learning Summer School

> **50th edition** of the Machine Learning Summer School  
> August 31 – September 11, 2026 (12 days)  
> Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Tübingen, Germany

## What is MLSS?

The [Machine Learning Summer School](http://mlss.cc/) is a prestigious annual series of summer schools on machine learning, originating in 2006. MLSS Tübingen is one of the flagship editions, held at MPI-IS — home to some of the world's leading ML researchers including **Bernhard Schölkopf** (co-founder of the series, founder of the ELLIS Institute Tübingen).

## Why This is Relevant

- **Cutting-edge ML theory** — MPI-IS is at the forefront of causal ML, kernel methods, probabilistic numerics, and representation learning
- **Networking with European PhD programs** — heavy ELLIS/FAIR/MPI-IS presence; great for PhD connections in Germany and Europe
- **Poster session** — accepted students typically present a poster; strong signal for research visibility
- **Bernhard Schölkopf involvement** — the organizer/scientific director is one of the most influential figures in modern ML
- **50th edition milestone** — historically more competitive and well-resourced

## Application Requirements

Based on the application portal (login required at /apply/) and past MLSS iterations:

1. **Online application form** — create account at mlss2026.is.tuebingen.mpg.de/apply/register/
2. **CV / Resume** — academic focus; ML research experience valued
3. **Motivation letter** — typically 1-2 pages explaining research interests and why MLSS
4. **Letter of recommendation** — 1 referee (academic, preferably ML/computational)
5. **Academic transcripts** — degree certificates and transcripts
6. **Poster abstract** (likely) — many MLSS editions require a poster proposal

### Standard Eligibility
- Graduate students (Masters, PhD)
- Postdocs and early-career researchers
- Strong interest in ML theory and applications

### Funding Options
- **DAAD scholarships** — German academic exchange; relevant for international students
- **University travel grants** — check home institution
- **MPI-IS visitor funding** — occasionally available for accepted students
- **ELLIS Foundation** — may have travel support for ELLIS scholars
- MLSS itself does **not** typically offer full funding, but many past attendees cobble together grants
